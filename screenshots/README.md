## Dashboard Screenshots

These screenshots represent key Power BI dashboard pages built on top of the SQL data model.

**Pages included:**
- Executive Overview – high-level KPIs and reimbursement performance
- Revenue Leakage – underpayments, overpayments, and unpaid claims
- Payment Timeliness – average days to payment and trends over time

The dashboards are powered by PostgreSQL reporting views and designed for executive and operational decision-making.
